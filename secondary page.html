About Me

Hello! I’m DestinyFaith Trujillo, a passionate graphic designer dedicated to crafting visuals that resonate with authenticity and intention. With a background in various design disciplines, I aim to create meaningful experiences through my work.

My design philosophy revolves around the belief that every project has a unique story to tell. I draw inspiration from nature, art, and the world around me, infusing my creations with earthy aesthetics and a touch of creativity.

As I embark on my journey as a designer, I am committed to continuous growth and exploration, striving to push boundaries and elevate the art of design. Join me as I share my vision and work through Echos of Intention.
